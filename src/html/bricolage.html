Creating Lamps

My passion for crafting lamps dates back to my school days when I saw Olafur Eliasson’s artwork “Red emotional globe” in an exhibition. I was so awestruck that I went straight home and started building a replica to have it always near. After this initiation, I dared to work on own creations. Since having a fully fledged workshop is not compatible with a student's lifestyle, I learned quickly adapting my ideas in order to bring them to life with limited resources. My works heavily rely on repurposing available materials and products - the base frames of the hexagonal lamp for example are built from sanitary pipes and wooden baseboards. Furthermore I make use of simple manufacturing techniques such as soldering.
